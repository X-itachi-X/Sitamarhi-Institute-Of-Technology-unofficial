Manjhar Kund

  • Manjhar Kund is a popular tourist attraction located in the state of Bihar, India.

  • Visitors can also explore the nearby caves and rock formations, adding to the adventure and exploration aspect of the visit.

  • The accessibility to Manjhar Kund has improved in recent years, with better roads and facilities, making it easier for tourists to reach the site.

  • It is recommended to carry snacks, drinking water, and sunscreen while visiting Manjhar Kund, as there may not be many food and beverage options available in the immediate vicinity.

  • The water falling from the waterfall forms a natural pool at the bottom, making it an ideal spot for swimming and enjoying the cool waters.

  • It is advisable to visit Manjhar Kund during the daytime as the area may not be well-lit during the evening or night.

  • It is situated near Dhua Kund
