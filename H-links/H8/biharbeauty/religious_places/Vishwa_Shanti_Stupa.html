Vishwa Shanti Stupa

  • Vishwa Shanti Stupa, also called the Peace Pagoda. It is located at the highest point of Ratnagiri hill, at an altitude of 400 m in Rajgir, qualifying its serene charm as divine.

  • Built completely with marble, Stupa comprises four golden statues of Lord Buddha with each representing his life periods of birth, enlightenment, preaching and death.

  • There is a ropeway which helps the tourists reach Vishwa Shanti Stupa a top Ratnagiri hill. The other way to reach this “Stupa” is a serpentine staircase.

  • It also has a small Japanese Buddhist temple with a large park. There is a temple near the stupa where prayers are conducted for universal peace.
