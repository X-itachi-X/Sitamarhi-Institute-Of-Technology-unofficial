Garib Nath Mandir

  • The 'Baba Garibnath Temple' located in Muzaffarpur city of Bihar is also known as 'Devghar' of the state. This temple is a symbol of faith and reverence. It is believed that all the wishes of the devotees asked with devotion in this temple are fulfilled.

  • A large number of devotees visit this temple in the month of Sawan. On the lines of Deoghar, Baba Garibnath Dham also has a tradition of performing Jalabhishek of Baba in just 12 hours by taking Dak Bomb Ganga water. Every year the number of kanwariyas is increasing by 10-15 percent.

  • The history of this temple is 300 years old. It is believed that earlier there used to be a dense forest at this place. There were seven peepal trees in the middle of this forest. At the time of felling of the tree, a red substance like blood suddenly started coming out and when this place was excavated, a huge Shivling was found from here. People tell that after this Lord Shiva appeared in the dream of the landowner. Since then Baba Bholenath was worshiped here.

  • People believe that there was a very poor person, he had a daughter. There was nothing in the house for daughter's marriage. One day a person saw Baba in a dream. After which all the wedding items were automatically supplied. Since then the name of this Dham has become 'Baba Garibnath'.
