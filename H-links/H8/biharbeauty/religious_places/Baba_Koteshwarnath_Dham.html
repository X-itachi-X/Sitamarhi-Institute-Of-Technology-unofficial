Baba Koteshwarnath Dham

  • The ancient Shiva temple Baba Koteshwar Nath Dham, which is the center of faith, is located at the confluence of Morhar village and Dardha rivers in Main village of Belaganj block, Jehanabad district of Bihar. Where Sahastra Lingi Mahadev is located in the sanctum sanctorum of Koteshwar Nath Temple.

  • The dome of the newly built temple with South Indian art is visible from the beginning of the village. The ancient huge Peepal tree located 100 meters north of the temple, whose trunk touching the earth and turning towards the temple, it seems that all the leaves of the tree are bowing down to Mahadev Koteshwar Nath.

  • The mention of this temple is also mentioned in the report of the Archaeological Survey of India. According to the Archaeological Survey Department, the Shivling present here is estimated to be of fifth and sixth century.

  • This is the place where Usha, the daughter of the demon Vanasura, fell in love with Aniruddha, the grandson of Lord Krishna, who was married by the self-manifested Lord Shiva. Usha used to worship Lord Shiva daily. Lord Shiva had to do this because of the worship of Usha.
