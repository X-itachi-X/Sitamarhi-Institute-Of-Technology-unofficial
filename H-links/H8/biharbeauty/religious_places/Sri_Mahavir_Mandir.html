Sri Mahavir Mandir

  • Mahavir Mandir is one of the holiest Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Hanuman in Patna, Bihar, India. It is one of the best and ancient Hanuman temples in the country. Mahavir Temple is the most famous temple in North India. Lakhs of devotees visit the temple every year. The statue of Sankat Mochan holds a special place in the hearts of the devotees. Many people come to this temple on the auspicious occasion of Ram Navami.

  • Mahavir Mandir, Patna is one of the leading Hanuman temples in the country. Thousands of devotees worshiped Shri Hanumanji and visited the temple. It is a manokamana temple, where every wish of the devotees is fulfilled, and this is the reason for the increasing number of devotees in the temple.

  • n 1948, the Patna High Court declared it a public temple. The new grand temple was constructed between 1983 AD and 1985 AD with the contribution of Hon'ble Acharya Kishore Kunal and his devotees, and is currently one of the world famous temples in the country.
