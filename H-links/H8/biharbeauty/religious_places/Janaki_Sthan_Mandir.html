Janki Sthan Mandir

  • About 1.5 km from railway station and bus stand. This is the birth place of Sita. Janaki-kund is in the south near the temple. Thousands of devotees throng the temple during Navratri and Rama Navami festivals. It is believed that this temple is about 100 years old.

  • The legend of this place refers to the epic Ramayana and is famous as the place where Sita was nursed under a shade in the filed where she was found as a new born born by King Janaka.

  • It is a fairly large temple with a modern structure. The main deities of the temple are Sri Rama, Sita and Hanuman. A nearby tank famous as Janaki Kund is the place where King Janak used to take his daughter Sita to bathe. This was the belief of popular mythology.
